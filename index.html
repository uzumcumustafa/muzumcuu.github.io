How to Interact with Our Chatbot Boss

1. Greet the Boss: Start by saying hello to receive your topic for a time-off prompt.
2. Write a Persuasive Prompt: Follow the given topic and craft a detailed, engaging prompt for the boss.
3. Aim for High Quality: Include specific details, ask the chatbot to adopt a persona, or provide step-by-step instructions to increase your score.
4. Watch Out for Feedback: The boss will rate your prompt with humor and a score. A score of 7 or higher means you get the day off!
5. Enjoy the Interaction: Even if you don't earn the day off, the experience is sure to bring a laugh or two.

Happy prompting!
